Intel’s Journey to a Sustainable Future

Explore Intel’s key sustainability milestones and future goals

2022: 100% Renewable Energy in U.S. Operations

Intel achieved 100% renewable electricity use in its U.S. operations, a major step toward global sustainability.

  • Reduced carbon footprint in U.S. manufacturing
  • Set a benchmark for global operations

2025: Net Positive Water Use

Intel aims to restore more water than it consumes in global operations by 2025.

  • Investing in water restoration projects
  • Partnering with local communities

2030: Zero Waste to Landfill

Intel is committed to sending zero waste to landfill from its global manufacturing operations by 2030.

  • Innovative recycling and reuse programs
  • Supplier engagement for waste reduction

2040: Net-Zero Greenhouse Gas Emissions

Intel’s long-term goal is to achieve net-zero greenhouse gas emissions across its global operations by 2040.

  • Investing in renewable energy worldwide
  • Innovating for energy-efficient products
